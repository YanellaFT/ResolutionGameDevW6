# ResolutionGameDevW6
This is a multiplayer game made in GameMaker for Resolution Game Dev Week 6.

It requires two players to play. 

* use WASD or the arrow keys to move your player
* point your mouse in the direction to shoot
* press the space bar or left on your mouse to shoot

Get the most kills to win!
